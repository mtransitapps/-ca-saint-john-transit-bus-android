This app adds Saint John Transit buses information to MonTransit.

This app provides the buses schedule and the latest news from @ThinkTransitSJ on Twitter.

Saint John Transit buses serve Saint John in New Brunswick, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link https://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the City of Saint John.
https://catalogue-saintjohn.opendata.arcgis.com/datasets/d6f4783521364429a2e51a64c60ae234/about

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-saint-john-transit-bus-android

This app is not related with Saint John Transit.

Permissions:
- Other: required to read the news from Twitter
